Serve to 4 people.
Cooking time=2 hours 30 minutes

Ingridients for Beshbarmak:
  • Lamb meat-1.5 kg
  • Carrot-1 piece
  • Water-2 liters
  • Dough
  • Onions-3 pieces
Instructions:
  1. Put meat, 1 onion, carrots in a saucepan, pour 2 liters of water, bring to a boil and remove the foam, cook the broth for 2-3 hours over low heat.
  2. Cut the dough into rectangles or rhombuses about 5 - 7 cm, leave the dough for 20 minutes to dry.
  3. From the finished broth, remove the onions, carrots and meat. Remove the meat from the bones and cut into small pieces.
  4. Dilute 1/2 liter of broth with water and bring to a boil, in this broth we will cook sliced ​​dough.Rectangles or rhombuses from the dough, cook until readiness in boiling broth in 2-3 calls, about 2 minutes. Finished remove the rectangles from the broth with a slotted spoon and put on a large plate.
  5. On them, lay the pieces of meat on top, and put the onion stewed in the broth on the meat.Serve the broth separately.And your meal is ready!
